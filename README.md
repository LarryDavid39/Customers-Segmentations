# Sales Analysis

### Project Overview

A project that groups customers into different segments: according to their age group, gender, occupation, income level, their loyalty and of course their location.

### Tool(S)

- PowerBI

### Data Cleaning/Preparation

We performed the following task in the initial data preparation phase:
1. Data loading and inspection.
2. Handling missing values.
3. Data cleaning and formatting.

### Exploratory Data Analysis(EDA)

EDA involves exploing the sales data to answer some key questions, such as:

1. What is the total number of customers?
2. What is the average age of these customers?
3. What is the average income of our customers?
4. How many customers do we have each year?
5. What is the average tenure of these customers?
6. What are their occupations, and how are they patronizing us?

### Result/Findings

The analysis result are summarized as follows:
- We had the most customers in the year 2017, and the least in 2015
- Most of our customers are females
- Majority of them came from the southern part of the country
